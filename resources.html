Helpful Resources

Here's a list of some of the best resources to help guide you on your weightlifting journey. From beginner guides to advanced programming, these tools can support your goals safely and effectively.

Beginner-Friendly Guides

  • Nerd Fitness - Great for beginners, with fun, easy-to-follow strength routines.
  • StrongLifts 5x5 - A simple, effective training program.

YouTube Channels

  • Jeff Nippard - Science-based lifting and nutrition advice.
  • MegSquats - Women's strength training and powerlifting.

Training Apps

  • Fitbod - Personalized strength training plans based on equipment and recovery.
  • Strong - Easy-to-use workout tracking app for lifters.

Books

  • Starting Strength by Mark Rippetoe
  • The New Rules of Lifting by Lou Schuler & Alwyn Cosgrove

Knowledge is power! Use these resources to educate yourself, track progress, and stay motivated.
